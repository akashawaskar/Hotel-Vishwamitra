Why Vishwamitra

ROOMS & SUITES

Offering impeccable style and modern convenience in equal measure, our rooms are beautifully furnished, ornamented with unique, modern art and equipped with the latest technologies.

WIFI UNLIMITED

Equipped with high speed WiFi access and served by a 24 hour personal butler, luxury accommodation at The Oberoi, Mumbai is designed to meet the needs of business and leisure travellers to the city.

LUXURIOUS FURNISHINGS

Each of our luxury accommodations in Latur are furnished with king or queen size beds, silk armchairs, a mother of pearl dresser and polished wooden desk. The walls are ornamented with antique lithographs and bespoke modern art.

CUTTING-EDGE TECHNOLOGY

All rooms are equipped with multiple telephones, desk drawer plug points, ceiling mounted reading lamps, a DVD player and televisions in the living spaces and bathroom.
