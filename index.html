About Vishwamitra

A Vishwamitra International boutique hotel is located in Latur’s hippest and trendiest area i.e. Nandi Stop, close to all commercial areas. Just 4KM from the Latur Railway Station, 2KM from MSRTC Bus Stand and 10KM from Latur Airport.

40 elegantly appointed rooms equipped with all the modern facilities like direct dialing of STD & ISD from rooms, Televisions, Wi-Fi Services, Mini Bars & more to fulfill the needs of the customers.

The background we believe in providing an outstanding guest experience for city explorers. Founded by MANE HOTELS PVT LTD who share a genuine love for travelers, Vishwamitra is an innovative, fast-growing hotel business that currently has location in Latur totaling 40 rooms and further locations coming on-stream from 2017.

Our accommodation is the talk of the town and is ideal for guests who want to be part of the creative and cutting-edge world that is center area of Latur. There are literally numbers of cool shopping centers, quirky boutiques and alternative coffee shops all around us.
